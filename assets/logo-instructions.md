# University Logo Instructions

Please upload the following logo files to the assets folder:

## Required Logo Files:

1. **semo-logo.png** - Southeast Missouri State University logo
   - Recommended size: 200x200px or higher
   - Format: PNG with transparent background preferred
   - Should be the official university logo

2. **vvit-logo.png** - Vasireddy Venkatadri Institute of Technology logo
   - Recommended size: 200x200px or higher
   - Format: PNG with transparent background preferred
   - Should be the official college logo

## Logo Placement:
- Logos will appear on the left side of the degree titles
- Size: 50px x 50px on desktop, 40px x 40px on mobile
- Background: White with subtle border
- Fallback: If logo fails to load, it will be hidden automatically

## Upload Instructions:
1. Save your university logos with the exact filenames above
2. Upload them to the assets folder
3. The website will automatically display them next to the university names
